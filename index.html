About Me

My Name is Taylor Chesnutt

I'm a web developer with experience building apps using Rails, React, and Javascript. As a Chemistry major in college I took many classes in Physical and Inorganic Chemistry as I loved the raw problem solving (though the labs were fun too). It was at A&N Technical Services that I first encountered coding after I taught myself a little R to make my job easier. I was hungry and wanted more so I took online coursework at night from nationally recognized Statistics.com. After a brief stint at App Acadamy, I’ve pursued my passion by building projects every week using frameworks such as React and Rails. I recently participated in a collaboration to build a synonym finder that works within Google Docs. By employing VanillaJS, we were able to minimize size and load time. I’ve found my chemist’s attention to detail has aided my programming in unexpected ways. I now seek an opportunity to continue to develop my coding skill set. I’m looking for a company who both has interesting challenges and the desire to help me grow professionally.
